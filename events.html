Events

We organise a number of clean up events in the Dublin area typically every second month. Each month usually has a theme such as parks, rivers, beaches, Tidy Towns etc and we welcome any suggestions you may have for areas that would benefit from a community spruce up! Everyone is welcome, you just need to turn up at the time and place noted below and we will provide all equipment. It is a great way to get involved, meet new people and know that you are having a positive impact on the local community and environment. Hopefully we see you at an event soon!
